# ERA — Website Context

## Company

**NPT Shtypshkronja ERA** is a Kosovo-based paper packaging manufacturer.
Family-owned, based in the Pristina region. Custom orders only — no standard catalog.
Everything is made from paper (Kraft, GD, various grams and sizes).

## Products

### 1. Paper Cups (Gota)
Standard paper cups custom-printed with client logos.
Sizes: 3.5oz, 7oz, 12oz.
Highest volume product — sold to cafes, restaurants, food businesses.

### 2. Pasta & Soup Cups (Kupa)
Larger paper cups for pasta, soup, and noodle portions.
Sizes: S, M, L + noodle/soup variant.
Highest revenue product category.

### 3. Ice Cream Cups (Kupa Akullore)
Paper cups for ice cream. Two sizes: H53 and H63.

### 4. Food Boxes (Kuti)
Custom printed packaging boxes for food businesses.
Variants: burger boxes, fries boxes, salad boxes, cake/sweets boxes, pate boxes, Dubai gold foil gift boxes.

### 5. Cutlery Holders (Mbajtese)
Paper sleeves/holders for spoons and coffee cups.
Types: spoon holders, 2-slot coffee holders, universal coffee holders.

### 6. Table Paper (Leter Tavoline)
Paper rolls used as disposable table covers in restaurants.

### 7. Wrapping Paper (Leter Mbeshtjellese)
Paper used for wrapping products.

### 8. Labels & Stickers (Etiketa)
Custom self-adhesive labels for products (olive oil, food, beverages, etc.)

### 9. Cardboard Packaging (Kartona)
Larger cardboard boxes for product packaging (spices, canned drinks, etc.)

### 10. Paper Plates (Pjata)
Disposable paper plates.

### 11. Cup Lids (Kapak Gota)
Lids for paper cups.
