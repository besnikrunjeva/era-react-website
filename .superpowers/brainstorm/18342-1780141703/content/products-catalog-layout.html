B — Category filter tabs

Filter tabs at top: "Kafe & Pije", "Ushqim", "Embëlsira", "Tjera". Client clicks their industry and sees only relevant products. 3D badge highlights interactive products.

Pros

  • Clients find their product by use case
  • Scales well as you add products

Cons

  • Slightly more complex to build

C — Dark two-zone layout

Dark ERA aesthetic. Top zone: horizontal scrollable strip of 3D-editor products (dark cards, green glow). Bottom zone: clean grid of box/other products. Matches the rest of the site's dark branding.

Pros

  • Consistent with site's dark aesthetic
  • 3D products feel dramatically different

Cons

  • No category filtering
