Which scroll animation style?

All three use your real GLB models. Pick the feel that matches what you had in mind.

A — Apple iPhone style

One 3D model stays fixed center-screen. As you scroll, text callouts reveal around it — each highlight pointing to a feature. Model slowly rotates. Very cinematic, very Apple.

Pros

  • Most premium feel
  • Product features feel intentional

Cons

  • Best with one hero model

B — Product parade

Each product gets its own "chapter" as you scroll. Gota flies in from left, Akullore from right, Mbajtëse from below — each with its own headline and description. Like flipping through a product lookbook.

Pros

  • Shows all 3 models equally
  • Great for products page

Cons

  • Longer scroll distance

C — Sticky scroll with model swap

One full-height sticky canvas. A progress bar on the side shows which product you're on. As you scroll through "chapters," the 3D model crossfades to the next product. Text slides in from right. Clean, fast, controlled.

Pros

  • Smooth, no layout jumps
  • Works on homepage + own page

Cons

  • Crossfade needs care
